Banana bread

Ingredients

  1. butter
  2. 2 very ripe bananas
  3. 140g self-raising flour
  4. handful dried banana chips
  5. caster sugar

steps

step 1: Heat oven to 180C/160C fan/gas step 2: Butter a 2lb loaf tin and line the base and sides with baking parchment.
step 3: Cream 140g softened butter and 140g caster sugar until light and fluffy, then slowly add 2 beaten large eggs with a little of the 140g flour.

The best spaghetti bolognese recipe

Ingredients

  1. 1 tbsp olive oil
  2. 4 rashers smoked streaky bacon
  3. 2 medium onions
  4. 2 carrots
  5. 500g beef mince
  6. 400g spaghetti
  7. 6 cherry tomatoes
  8. 1 red chilli

steps

step 1: Put a large saucepan on a medium heat and add 1 tbsp olive oil. step 2: Add 4 finely chopped bacon rashers and fry for 10 mins until golden and crisp. step 3: Reduce the heat and add the 2 onions, 2 carrots, 2 celery sticks, 2 garlic cloves and the leaves from 2-3 sprigs rosemary, all finely chopped, then fry for 10 mins. Stir the veg often until it softens.
