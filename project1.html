Entencity Detail

Not only is Madagascar one of the most exotic places to travel, it’s also one of the most unique places on Earth. This island country is home to otherworldly landscapes such as limestone karsts, plunging canyons, pristine white beaches, and captivating rainforests. These habitats are home to an amazing variety of wildlife, 90% of which don’t exist anywhere else on Earth. Head to one of Madagascar’s many national parks to spot lemurs, swim with whale sharks off the island of Nosy Be, and go whale watching off Île Sainte-Marie. In between lounging on the beach and sampling local cuisine in the capital of Antananarivo, make time to visit the aptly named Forest of Knives - a jaw-dropping landscape of vertical limestone rocks interspersed with canyons, w aterfalls, and forest.
